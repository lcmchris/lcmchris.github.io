Tag but in real life.

Yesterday, when I was heading home from a long day. In the corner crossing of Camden road, I saw someone whip out a phone and snapped a picture of their friend across the street. This got me thinking about the possibility of an augmented reality tag game.

IDEA: AR Tag

People running around a fixed area with a target and a tagger. Tagging is snapping a clear picture of your target. Tagging out someone gives you power ups. Certain chests are available for power ups. These power ups encourage more movement and make it not a hide and seek (more like a shooting game). Power ups to include, freezing users, radar for your target and untaggability.

This balances the game to encourage running around. Winner is the last man standing.
